Job Board

Web Developer

A web developer is responsible for developing websites and ensuring that they are functioning correctly. They should have experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Graphic Designer

A graphic designer is responsible for creating visual concepts, using computer software or by hand, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or captivate consumers. They should have experience with Adobe Creative Suite and other design tools.

Marketing Manager

A marketing manager is responsible for creating and executing marketing campaigns. They should have experience in market research, advertising, and brand management.
